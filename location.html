How to Reach Barra Inn

By Air

From Glasgow to Barra

Fly from Glasgow International Airport to Barra Airport, landing on the sandy beach of Traigh Mhòr.
Flight time: 1 hour.

By Sea

From Oban to Castlebay

Ferries run from Oban to Castlebay, Barra’s main village, with scenic views of the Hebridean islands.
Crossing time: 5 hours. Castlebay is a short drive from Barra Inn.

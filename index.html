ISLE OF BARRA

Welcome to the Barra Inn, a tranquil escape perched above Tangasdale beach.
Fall asleep to the sound of wild Atlantic waves, wake to ocean views, fresh air, and Hebridean calm.
Just a few minutes away from Castlebay, our unique guesthouse offers nature at your doorstep: dolphins, volcanic rocks, wild meadow flowers and stunning sunsets.
Whether you're strolling into town or sharing beach walks with your dog, this is your island home.

A Story in Every Sunset

Barra is where wild beauty meets quiet wonder.

Crystal-clear waters, rolling waves, and golden sunsets set the rhythm of life. Each evening feels like the closing chapter of a story you’ll want to read again. On the clearest nights, the northern lights ripple above the sea, turning the sky into a living canvas.

Whether you’re climbing a grassy hilltop or walking barefoot across silver sands, Barra is a place to simply be.

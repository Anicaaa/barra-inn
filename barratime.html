ISLE OF BARRA

Kisimul Castle

Kisimul Castle, known as the “Castle in the Sea,” is one of Barra’s highlights. In summer, you can take a short boat trip around the castle and enjoy the views. Nearby, there are cafés, pubs, and restaurants where you can relax with food and drinks.

Vatersay

Vatersay, reached by a short causeway from Barra, is a place of beautiful beaches and open views. In summer, you may spot cows relaxing on the sand while you enjoy the scenery. The island is also known for its sunsets, and the Vatersay Gate makes a great stop for taking in the peaceful surroundings.

Barra Airport

Barra Airport is one of the island’s most unique sights, with planes landing directly on the beach at low tide. It’s the only airport of its kind in the world, making it a popular spot for plane-watching. Visitors often gather nearby to watch the landings and take in the unusual setting.
